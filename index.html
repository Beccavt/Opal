Who is The Opal?

"Opal" by Jack Stauber is this dreamy, slightly unhinged little animated short that feels like a weird childhood memory you can’t fully explain. It mixes creepy and cute in a way that’s honestly kind of eerie but also beautiful. The music and visuals feel nostalgic, like flipping through forgotten old memories that are soft around the edges but deeply emotional. It’s the kind of thing that makes you sit there after and just think… What did I just watch, and why do I feel something!?
